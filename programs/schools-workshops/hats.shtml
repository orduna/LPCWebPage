Hands-on Advanced Tutorial Sessions at the LPC - HATS@LPC and LPC Academic Lectures

The schedule for upcoming and past HATS@LPC and LPC academic lectures is maintained in this page. Details on each tutorial, including an Indico page with a description will be announced as the event gets closer. All HATS@LPC sessions will take place in the Sunrise 11th floor conference room (WH11NE) at the LPC. Lectures may be occasionally hosted in larger conference rooms to allow for wider participation. Advanced (no cost) registration will be requested for participation in these events.
